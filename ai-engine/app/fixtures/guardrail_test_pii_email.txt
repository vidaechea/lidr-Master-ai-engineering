Build an admin portal for managing customer accounts. The project owner is john.doe@example.com and they need user authentication with SSO support (Google and Microsoft), a customer list with search and filtering, a detail view for each customer with their current plan and usage metrics, the ability to upgrade or downgrade a plan, an audit log of all admin actions, and basic role-based access control with two roles (admin and read-only). Backend will reuse the existing PostgreSQL database via a thin REST API. Frontend in React. Target launch in six weeks.
